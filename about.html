BIO-PACKS provides high quality biodegradable packaging materials to the consumers whos business require the usage of packaging materials whether it may be a small time ecommerce business to courier service companies.

The main purpose of Bio-Packs is to reduce the amount of waste produced due to plastic packaging materials used by everyday Malaysians.
